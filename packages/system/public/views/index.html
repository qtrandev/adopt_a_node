Adopt-a-City Street Program

Neighborhoods, businesses and individuals can make a difference in their community by adopting a City Street. Interested parties can view the interactive map displaying the City streets that have been adopted and those that are eligible for adoption. .

Program Highlights Include:

  • Applicants are limited to one street adoption at a time for the first year
  • Only city maintained streets can be adopted, to learn about state road adoption, please visit NCDOT
  • A minimum of three (3) clean ups are required/year for a two-year commitment
  • Volunteers receive two signs along their adopted route
  • Volunteers must submit a cleanup report for each clean up
  • Volunteers are asked to remove illegal signs along the roadway during the clean up
  • Additional opportunities include storm drain marking and graffiti spotting
  • Keep Charlotte Beautiful provides bags, safety vests and litter sticks

For more information about the program, download the Adopt-a-City Street One Page Info Sheet
